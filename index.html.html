Here are the core roles found in most businesses and what they're responsible for:

Executive Leadership

  • CEO – Sets overall vision and strategy, makes major decisions, represents the company externally
  • COO – Oversees day-to-day operations and ensures business functions run efficiently
  • CFO – Manages financial planning, risk, reporting, and capital allocation
  • CTO/CIO – Leads technology strategy, infrastructure, and innovation

Finance & Accounting

  • Controller – Manages accounting operations, financial reporting, and compliance
  • Accountants/Bookkeepers – Record transactions, manage payroll, handle taxes, and maintain ledgers
  • Financial Analysts – Forecast performance, analyze data, and support budgeting decisions

Human Resources

  • HR Manager – Oversees recruiting, onboarding, benefits, compliance, and workplace culture
  • Recruiters – Source, interview, and hire talent
  • Payroll Specialists – Process compensation and ensure regulatory compliance

Sales

  • Sales Manager – Sets targets, manages the sales team, and drives revenue strategy
  • Account Executives – Prospect, pitch, and close new business
  • Account Managers – Maintain and grow existing client relationships

Marketing

  • Marketing Manager – Plans campaigns, manages brand, and drives lead generation
  • Content/Creative – Produces copy, design, and multimedia assets
  • Digital/Performance Marketers – Manages paid ads, SEO, email, and analytics

Operations

  • Operations Manager – Optimizes workflows, manages vendors, and improves efficiency
  • Project Managers – Plan, execute, and deliver projects on time and within budget
  • Logistics/Supply Chain – Manages inventory, procurement, and distribution

Customer Service

  • Customer Service Manager – Sets support standards and manages the team
  • Support Representatives – Handle customer inquiries, complaints, and issue resolution

Legal & Compliance

  • General Counsel/Legal – Manages contracts, risk, regulatory compliance, and litigation
  • Compliance Officers – Ensure the business adheres to laws and internal policies

IT & Technology

  • IT Manager – Maintains systems, networks, security, and hardware
  • Developers/Engineers – Build and maintain software, tools, and platforms
  • Cybersecurity – Protects data and systems from threats

Product (for product-based businesses)

  • Product Manager – Defines product vision, roadmap, and prioritization
  • UX/UI Designers – Design user experiences and interfaces
  • QA/Testers – Ensure product quality before release

The exact mix depends on the industry and company size — small businesses often combine several of these roles into one person, while larger organizations build full teams around each function.
